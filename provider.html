Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a lead?

A lead is counted when a customer clicks the "Call Now" button on your listing and the call connects. You are never charged for page views or impressions.

Is there a monthly fee?

No. Zero monthly fees. You only pay when you receive a call from a customer.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts. You can pause or remove your listing at any time with zero penalty.

How do I know leads are real?

Our click tracking system logs every call. You can view your lead count in real-time via the provider dashboard. If a lead seems suspicious, contact us and we'll review it.

What if I get too many calls?

You can set a daily or monthly lead cap. Once you reach your limit, your listing shows "Currently at capacity" and calls pause until the next period.

Which cities and services are available?

We cover 505 cities across all 50 states. Every emergency service category is open — plumbing, electrical, HVAC, locksmith, mechanic, roofing, appliance repair, pest control, and water damage restoration.
