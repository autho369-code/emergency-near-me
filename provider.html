Common Questions

What does "5 spots per city" mean?

Each service category in each city has exactly 5 listing slots. Once all 5 are filled, no more providers can join for that service in that city. First come, first served. Your competitors who don't sign up simply won't appear on our pages.

What if a customer calls but doesn't hire me?

That's fine. You pay a flat monthly fee — not per call or per lead. The fee stays the same whether you get 10 calls or 100. Your job is to close the leads you receive.

Can I see my traffic and call data?

Yes. Every listing includes click tracking. Your monthly report shows how many customers clicked to call, peak times, and service demand patterns.

What happens if I cancel?

Your listing is removed within 24 hours. The slot opens up for another provider. No penalties, no questions asked. You can rejoin at any time if the slot is still available.

Can I list multiple services in the same city?

Yes. The Pro plan allows up to 3 service categories (e.g., you can be the exclusive plumber AND HVAC provider for Chicago). Enterprise plans have no limits.

How do I know my territory won't be taken?

Once you're listed, your territory is locked. We never sell your slot to a competitor as long as your subscription is active. First-come, first-served.
