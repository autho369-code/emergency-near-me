Answers

Common questions

What does "5 providers per city" mean?

Each city and service category has only five available provider placements. Once a city/service is full, additional providers must join the waitlist or choose a different territory.

Do customers go to my website or contact me through CallNowService?

You can route customers directly to your business using your website, emergency service page, booking page, contact page, or phone number.

Can I use my own emergency service page instead of my homepage?

Yes. If you already have a dedicated emergency or city-specific service page, we can use that as your preferred destination.

What happens if my city is already full?

You can join the waitlist, choose a nearby city, or ask about a broader territory plan.

Can I claim multiple cities or multiple services?

Yes. Growth, Pro, and Enterprise plans are designed for providers expanding into multiple territories or service categories.

Do I pay per lead?

No. CallNowService uses flat monthly pricing, not per-lead charges or bidding wars.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans can be canceled anytime according to the plan terms.
